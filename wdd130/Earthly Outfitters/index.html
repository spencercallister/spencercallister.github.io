Trade

The fashion industry is responsible for 10 percent of our global emissions. This means that fast fabric producers like H&M, Target, Forever 21 and other major producers are harming our environment by the sheer amount of fabric they produce.

Together we can eliminate the amount of fabric produced to help lower the damage the fashion industry has made on the Earth. Here at Earthly Outfitters, you can assist by buying clothing already worn rather than increasing the already large demand for clothing in our society. We ensure all our fabric is in a high or medium quality state, so it is still as if you are buying it brand new!

We Help

Along with our business, we also offer volunteer opportunities to help clean up the earth a bit in the Teton Idaho Wyoming area. During these outings we collect trash in our national parks and other nature reserves to benefit the fauna and flora. We partner up with local organizations to clean the Earth and invite individuals and other companies to make a pledge to make one step at a time towards a healthier planet. Here on our site, you can sign up to join others in our community who want to keep the planet clean and make a difference one step at a time. If you want to volunteer, use the “Join Us” box on our “Volunteer” section to join us on our next adventure. Fighting climate change is a process that is done community by community and this is how we are helping ours.
